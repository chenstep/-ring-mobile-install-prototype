Frequently asked questions

General Installation Questions

What is Professional Installation for the Ring Mobile Security Tower?

Professional Installation is an optional service that provides fully managed delivery coordination, on-site installation, and system activation of your Ring Mobile Security Tower. A trained installation technician will assemble your unit, connect all components, and verify full system functionality — so your security tower is ready to use when they leave.

Do I need Professional Installation?

While DIY setup is available, the Ring Mobile Security Tower ships in up to 4 separate shipments (including a 300 lb. unit on a pallet, solar panels, trailer, and battery accessories depending on your configuration). Professional Installation is recommended for customers who prefer expert handling of heavy equipment and system configuration.

Is Professional Installation available in my area?

Professional Installation is available across all 48 contiguous United States. Once you submit your installation request, a technician will be assigned based on your location and current availability. If you are located outside the contiguous U.S. (e.g., Alaska, Hawaii, or U.S. territories), Professional Installation is not available at this time.

How much does Professional Installation cost?

Pricing depends on your Ring Mobile Security Tower configuration:

  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Plug-in: $499
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Solar Kit + Battery: $669
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Plug-in (with Trailer): $869
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Solar Kit + Battery + Trailer Kit: $999

Payment is made directly to the installation vendor — not through Amazon or Ring.

How long does Professional Installation take?

Installation time varies by configuration:

  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Plug-in: Less than 1 hour
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Solar Kit + Battery: Approximately 2 hours
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Plug-in (with Trailer): Approximately 3.5 hours
  • Ring Mobile Security Tower – Solar Kit + Battery + Trailer Kit: Approximately 4.5 hours

Please note: These are estimates only. Actual installation time may vary based on factors such as technician experience, weather conditions, site location, ground surface, accessibility, and other environmental considerations. Your technician will provide an estimated completion time upon arrival based on your specific configuration and site conditions.

Scheduling & Preparation

How do I sign up for Professional Installation?

On this product page, click the "Professional Installation" link to complete a short 3-step sign-up form with your name, contact information, address, and installation details. You can also opt in after purchase — a confirmation email will be sent with a link to request installation.

When can I schedule my installation?

Your installation will be scheduled after ALL shipments for your order have been delivered. Once everything arrives, you'll receive an email notification with an equipment checklist and instructions to call the installation vendor directly to book your preferred date and time.

What should I have ready before the installer arrives?

Before your appointment, please ensure:

  • All delivered shipments/components are accounted for and accessible
  • A clear workspace at the installation location
  • Access to the installation area for the technician
  • You or an authorized adult present on-site (unless you selected Contactless Installation)

Can I choose a Contactless (Flexible) Installation?

Yes. When scheduling, you can choose between:

  • Standard Installation – You are present during installation; the technician explains each step and provides a hands-on demonstration upon completion.
  • Contactless (Flexible) Installation – You do not need to be present. The technician completes installation independently and contacts you via phone or video call for a walkthrough and sign-off.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes. Cancellations and reschedules are permitted up to 24 hours before your appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a trip fee, or you may convert to a Contactless Installation instead.

Installation Day

What happens during Professional Installation?

Your technician will:

  • Review your components and confirm your configuration
  • Assemble and install the Ring Mobile Security Tower per standard procedures
  • Test full system functionality
  • Walk you through the system and answer questions (Standard Installation), or contact you for a remote walkthrough (Contactless Installation)

What if a part is damaged or missing during installation?

Technicians carry backup stock of common replacement parts (cables, connectors, mounting hardware). If a part is unavailable on-site, you will be notified, a replacement will be shipped, and the vendor will schedule a return visit at no additional charge to you.

After Installation

How will I know my installation was completed correctly?

Every installation undergoes a quality assurance (QA) verification process. Your technician submits field documentation that is reviewed for install quality. Once verified, your system is activated and you'll receive a post-installation confirmation email.

Will the installer help me set up the Ring app?

Yes. For Standard Installations, the technician provides a hands-on demonstration and walks you through key features. For Contactless Installations, you'll receive a setup guide via email, with a follow-up call offered within 24 hours.

Support

Who do I contact if I have issues after installation?

Contact the Ring Customer Service team via phone, email, chat, or the Ring app. Support contact information is included in your post-installation email and within the Ring app. If an on-site follow-up is needed, it will be coordinated for you.

Will I be charged for follow-up visits?

  • If the issue is related to an installation defect, the follow-up visit is at no charge.
  • If the issue is customer-caused (e.g., the unit was relocated or damaged after installation), a service fee may apply.
