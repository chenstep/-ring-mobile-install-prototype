Frequently asked questions

General installation questions

How can I find a professional to install my Ring device?

After purchasing your Ring device on Amazon, you can find a professional installer using our locator tool above. Simply enter your zip code to see a list of installation professionals in your area.

Do I need professional installation for my Ring device?

While most Ring devices are designed for easy self-installation, some customers prefer professional installation for optimal placement, proper wiring, or integration with existing systems. Professional installation is recommended for more complex setups or if you're not comfortable with DIY installation. Installation by a qualified electrician may be required in your area. Refer to your local laws and building codes before performing electrical work; permits and/or professional installation may be required by law.

How much does professional installation cost?

Installation costs vary depending on your location, the type of Ring device, and the complexity of the installation. When you locate a professional through our dealer locator, you can contact them directly for a price quote before scheduling.

Scheduling and preparation

How do I schedule an installation appointment?

After finding an installer through our dealer locator tool, you can contact them directly using the provided phone number or email address to schedule an appointment at a convenient time.

What should I have ready before the installer arrives?

Before your appointment, please have:

  • Your Ring device and all included components
  • Your purchase receipt or order number
  • Access to your home Wi-Fi network and password
  • The Ring app downloaded on your mobile device
  • Clear access to the installation area

How long does professional installation typically take?

Most Ring device installations take between 30-60 minutes, depending on the device type and complexity. Doorbell installations that require wiring may take longer than battery-powered camera installations.

Post-purchase questions

I just purchased a Ring device on Amazon. When can I schedule installation?

You can schedule installation as soon as you receive your Ring device. We recommend having the device in hand before scheduling so the installer can complete the setup in one visit.

Can I purchase installation services at the same time as my Ring device on Amazon?

Currently, installation services are not bundled with Ring device purchases on Amazon. You'll need to arrange installation separately through our dealer locator after receiving your device.

What if I need installation for multiple Ring devices?

Typically, installers can handle multiple device installations in a single appointment. When contacting an installer, be sure to mention all devices you need installed so they can provide an accurate time and cost estimate.

What to expect from your installer

Will the installer help me set up the Ring app?

Yes, most installers will assist with basic app setup, including connecting your device to Wi-Fi and ensuring proper functionality before completing the installation.

Can installers help with integrating Ring with other smart home systems?

Many installers can assist with basic integration of Ring devices with compatible smart home systems like Alexa. For complex integrations, ask the installer about their capabilities before scheduling.

What if I'm not satisfied with the installation?

If you experience any issues with your installation, contact your installer directly. If you need additional assistance, Ring Customer Support is available to help resolve any concerns.
