Frequently Asked Questions

What does professional installation include?

Professional installation includes delivery, physical setup of your Ring Mobile Security Tower, network configuration, Ring app pairing, and a walkthrough of your system's features. The technician will ensure all cameras and sensors are functioning correctly before completing the installation.

How long does installation take?

A standard installation typically takes 2-4 hours depending on the configuration. Installations with solar kits or trailer setups may require additional time for proper alignment and calibration.

What site requirements are needed?

The installation site should have a relatively flat surface, adequate clearance for the tower height, and cellular coverage for the unit's connectivity. For solar kit configurations, an unobstructed sky view is recommended. Our technician will assess the site upon arrival.

Can I reschedule my installation?

Yes, you can reschedule your installation up to 24 hours before the scheduled appointment. Contact our installation support team or reply to your confirmation email to arrange a new time.

What happens after I submit this form?

After submission, a certified installer in your area will be assigned to your request. They will contact you within 2 business days to schedule a convenient installation date and time. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.
